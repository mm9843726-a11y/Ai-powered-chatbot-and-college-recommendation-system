Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common TNEA doubts

How is the TNEA cutoff calculated? ▼
TNEA cutoff = Maths (×1) + Physics (×0.5) + Chemistry (×0.5). Maximum is 200. Biology students use Biology instead of Maths.
What is the difference between Dream, Ambitious, and Safe colleges? ▼
Dream — predicted cutoff is above your score (stretch goal). Ambitious — within ±5 marks (realistic). Safe — your score is comfortably above predicted cutoff (high probability). These are planning guides, not guarantees.
Is the prediction 100% accurate? ▼
No. Predictions are based on historical TNEA cutoff data and an ML model. Actual cutoffs change every year. Always verify with the official TNEA portal.
Can I change my branch after allotment? ▼
Yes, during supplementary rounds if seats are available. Internal branch upgrades can be requested in some colleges after joining.
How many choices can I fill during TNEA counselling? ▼
There is no fixed limit. You can fill as many college-branch combinations as you wish. Advisable to fill at least 15–20 choices covering all three tiers.
