📋 Before You Begin — Important Notice

Please read these terms carefully. They explain how our predictions work and their limitations.

⚠️

Predictions Are Not Guarantees

Our ML model predicts cutoffs based on historical TNEA data (past 3 years). Actual cutoffs change every year depending on the number of applicants, seat availability, and reservation quotas. A college listed as "Safe" does NOT guarantee admission.

📉

Safe ≠ Confirmed; Dream ≠ Impossible

"Safe" means your score is historically above the predicted cutoff — but cutoffs can rise. "Dream" means you're below the typical cutoff — but if fewer students apply, it can fall. Treat all categories as planning guides, not definitive answers.

📊

Based on Previous Years' Records

All cutoff data is sourced from Anna University official TNEA records from previous counselling cycles. Seat matrix changes, new reservations, or policy updates from this year may not be reflected in our model.

🔢

Community Cutoffs Vary Significantly

Cutoffs differ widely between OC, BC, MBC, SC, SCA and ST categories. Always select your exact community for accurate predictions. Using the wrong community will produce incorrect results.

📍

Location Filter Uses College Name

Our dataset does not have a separate city column. The location filter searches city names embedded within college names (e.g., "Coimbatore" in "PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore"). Results may not cover all colleges in a city if the city name is not in the college's official name.

✅

Always Verify with Official TNEA Portal

This tool is for planning and guidance only. Before finalising your choice list, always cross-check cutoffs, seat availability, and allotment status at tneaonline.org — the only official source.

🤖

College Explorer Uses AI-Generated Data

College profiles in the Explorer section are generated by an AI model (GPT-4o-mini) based on its training data — not fetched from a live database. Placement figures, rankings, and other details are AI-estimated and may not be 100% accurate.
